The Ridge

The Ridge is a neighborhood located in Saratoga Springs, Utah. It was established in 2019 and is still growing!

Welcome to the Presenters Club at The Ridge! We meet every second Tuesday of the month. Our presenters are usually, but no always members of our neighborhood.

The types of things we learn include:

  • Health & Beauty
  • Cooking, Baking and Meal plannning
  • Home Care
