The Ridge

The Ridge is a neighborhood located in Saratoga Springs, Utah. It was established in 2019 and is still growing!

Welcome to the Presenters Club at The Ridge!

We meet on Tuesdays throughought each month. Our presenters are all women in our neighborhood who are generously volunteering their time and talents to teach us!

Fees are generally $5, but can vary.

The types of things we learn include:

  • Health & Beauty
  • Cooking, Baking and Meal plannning
  • Home Care
